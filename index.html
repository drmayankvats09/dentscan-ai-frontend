Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · hello@aayu365.com

1. Who We Are

Aayu AI is a free AI-powered dental health information service operated by Aayu365. We are committed to complete transparency about how we handle your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

2. What We Collect

When you use Aayu AI, we collect only what you voluntarily share:

  • Health information — symptoms, age, gender, medical history, medications, allergies
  • Dental photos you choose to upload for visual evaluation
  • Conversation content, used solely to generate your personalised assessment
  • Basic anonymous usage analytics to improve reliability
  • Contact information only if you reach out directly

We do not collect your data silently. Everything we know about you, you told us directly.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to generate your personalised AI dental assessment, improve our AI system using anonymised data, respond to support requests, and comply with Indian data protection regulations. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data. We never will.

4. DPDP Act 2023 Compliance

We operate in full compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023. Your health data is treated as sensitive personal data and processed only with your explicit, informed consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting hello@aayu365.com.

5. Data Processing & Storage

All conversation data is processed through our secure backend infrastructure with end-to-end encryption. We do not share your personal health data with any external service for commercial purposes. Anonymised, aggregated data may be used solely to improve our AI and contribute to public health research.

6. Data Security

All data is encrypted using TLS 1.3. We implement industry-standard security practices including strict access controls and regular security reviews. We will notify you promptly if a breach affecting your personal information occurs.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain conversation data for a limited period to improve AI accuracy. You may request complete deletion of all your personal data at any time by emailing hello@aayu365.com. All deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

8. Your Rights Under DPDP 2023

  • Right to Access — Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction — Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure — Request complete deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Opt out at any time
  • Right to Grievance — File a complaint with us or India's Data Protection Board

9. Medical Disclaimer

⚠️ Aayu AI provides general health information and AI-guided triage ONLY. It does not constitute medical diagnosis, prescription, or treatment advice. Aayu365 shall not be liable for any health outcomes based on AI-generated content. Always consult a licensed dental professional.

10. Contact Us

For all privacy concerns: hello@aayu365.com · Aayu365 · New Delhi, India
